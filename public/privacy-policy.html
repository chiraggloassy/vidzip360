Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

VidZip360 is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our video compression services:

  • Video Files: The videos you upload for compression are processed temporarily and automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • Technical Data: File size, format, duration, and compression settings
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous data about feature usage and performance metrics
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address (anonymized), and operating system

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Processing and compressing your video files
  • Improving our compression algorithms and service quality
  • Ensuring platform security and preventing abuse
  • Providing customer support when needed

3. Data Storage & Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Uploaded videos are stored temporarily and automatically deleted within 24 hours
  • Compressed videos are available for download for 24 hours before deletion
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
  • No personal information is stored beyond what's necessary for service operation
  • We don't sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to enhance our platform:

  • Cloud Storage: Temporary file storage with encrypted servers
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve user experience
  • CDN Services: Content delivery networks for faster file processing

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access information we have about you
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Request information about our data practices

6. Children's Privacy

VidZip360 does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
