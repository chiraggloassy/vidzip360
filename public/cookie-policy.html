Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

VidZip360 uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Performance Cookies: Help improve the performance of our Service

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Session Cookies
Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used to maintain your session and preferences during your visit.
Persistent Cookies
Remain on your device for a set period. Used to remember your preferences and settings for future visits.
Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how users interact with our website through anonymous data collection.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: For understanding website traffic and user behavior
  • Cloudflare: For security and performance optimization
  • CDN Providers: For efficient content delivery

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

Browser Settings: You can typically find cookie settings in the "Options" or "Preferences" menu of your browser.

Opt-out Tools: Various third-party tools are available to help you manage cookies across multiple websites.

6. What Happens If You Disable Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of VidZip360 may not function properly:

  • Your preference settings may not be saved
  • You may need to re-enter information multiple times
  • Certain features may not work as intended
  • The overall user experience may be affected

7. Changes to Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Cookie Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
